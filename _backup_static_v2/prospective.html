For Prospective Students

For Ph.D. Students

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students to join our team. Contact me if you are interested in the topics of the following areas:

  • System and Software Security
  • Hardware-assisted Security
  • Cloud, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Autonomous Driving Security
  • Deep learning and AI Security

Ideal Candidate Requirements

An ideal candidate is expected to have:

  • Strong motivation for doing research.
  • An earned master/bachelor degree in CS, EE, or other engineering majors.
  • Strong C/C++/Java/Python system programming experience.
  • Solid background in system programming and algorithms.
  • Solid background in Linux.
  • Solid background in Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, Data Structure, and Compiler.
  • Strong technical writing skills.
  • Previous research experience in information security, cryptography, and Operating Systems is a big plus.

For Master and Undergraduate Students

Several paid positions are available for Master and/or Bachelor students. Please send me (liua at oakland dot edu) the following documents in one email:

  • Your resume (mandatory)
  • Your publication (mandatory)
  • Your transcripts (mandatory)
  • Your TOEFL/GRE scores (mandatory if you are an international student)
  • Your software repositories (e.g., Github and/or Bitbucket) (optional)
  • Your personal statement (optional)
