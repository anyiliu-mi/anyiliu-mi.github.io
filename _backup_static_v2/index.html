About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Oakland University. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at George Mason University.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D./Master students with strong security and systems construction background (e.g., Virtualization, Networking, and OS). If you are interested in pursuing a Ph.D. under my supervision, please email me your CV, transcripts, and publications. Visiting scholars and capable graduate/undergraduate students are also welcome.
