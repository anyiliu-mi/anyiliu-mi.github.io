Research

Overview

My research interests lie in information security and privacy. I am building intrusion detection systems that are fast, reliable, and can detect real-world exploits, malware, and attacks. In particular, I focus on the following subjects:

  • Security, Privacy, and Trust Management
  • System and Software Security
  • Hardware-assisted Execution Environment
  • Cloud, Mobile, and IoT/CPS Security
  • Intrusion Detection, Prevention, and Correlation
  • Steganography and Side-Channel
  • Digital Forensics
  • Cybersecurity Education
